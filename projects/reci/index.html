Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook pancetta until crisp, about 8-10 minutes. Add garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, and Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Remove skillet from heat and add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.
  5. Add the egg and cheese mixture to the skillet with the spaghetti and toss quickly to combine. If the pasta seems dry, add a little bit of the reserved pasta water to help loosen it up.
  6. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  7. Divide spaghetti carbonara among plates and garnish with chopped parsley.
